Read More About Me

I'm currently a junior at Kent State University, where I'm majoring in Computer Science. Last fall, I began to work at a business kick-starter incubator known as KSU's Blackstone LaunchPad office. There, I'm the 3D printer director, and I'm also in charge of the check-out process for Arduino and Raspberry Pi Model kits. This summer I have been working as a software Co-Op for RoviSys in Aurora Ohio. Throughout this experience, I've had the chance to work with a variety of API's for implementation purposes, and I am currently in the beginning stages of writing code in C Sharp and JavaScript. All of my projects can be found on my GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.

Skills Acquired:

I know the basics of HTML. CSS. JavaScript. Python. Java. C++. Android Development. 3D Modeling. 3D Printing.

hacKSU

Kent Hack Enough

This year Kent Hack Enough will be hosted on the weekend of October 24th-26th. The goal is to try to have around 300-400 students participate in our hack. We encourage participants from all fields of study to attend, which fosters a good creative balance for teams.
